Community Clean-Up Drive Success

November 15, 2025
Community

Our recent community clean-up drive was a tremendous success with over 200 volunteers participating in the event. The initiative aimed to beautify our barangay and promote environmental awareness among residents.

Participants gathered at the barangay hall at 7:00 AM and were divided into groups to cover different areas of our community. The clean-up focused on collecting plastic waste, clearing drainage systems, and planting ornamental plants in public spaces.

Barangay Captain Juan Dela Cruz expressed his gratitude to all volunteers: "This shows the true spirit of bayanihan in our community. When we work together, we can achieve great things for our barangay."

The event concluded with a simple merienda for all participants and a commitment to conduct similar activities quarterly.
